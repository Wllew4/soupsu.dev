Extra Life 2019

Extra Life is a challenge where you livestream for 24 hours to raise money for your local Children Miracle Network hospital.

November 2, 2019 was our third year participating in Extra Life. This year, we invited more people to participate in the stream, making it easily our most fun year yet. We absolutely smashed our original goal of $200, raising a total of $1,169.10.

Unfortunately, the VOD for this stream was lost, but we have lots of great clips!
